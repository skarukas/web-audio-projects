Generative Spectral Music!

Volume


How It Works

  1. Every second, the fundamental frequency of all currently sounding frequencies is guessed.*
  2. A random harmonic (2 <= n <= 9) of this fundamental is generated. If there aren't any existing frequencies, a random frequency is generated instead.
  3. This new frequency is normalized to the range of 256-1024 Hz using some weird math.
  4. Repeat!

Each tone generated lasts between 2 and 5 seconds, and the fundamental frequency will slowly evolve as certain notes appear and disappear. They'll have a slight pitch bend 5% of the time, just for fun.

*This is calculated quick and dirty, by finding an unreliable sort of GCD (greatest common divisor) of a set of floating-point numbers. The fundamental will occasionally be erroneously calculated as a low number (< 5 Hz) meaning the generated tones may be very close together and dissonant. Sorry :(
